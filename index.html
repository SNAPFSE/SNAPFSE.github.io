Welcome to SNAP!
The Special Needs Assistance program for Social Engagement was created to increase the engagement, awareness, and communication between neurodiverse and neurotypical people and bring them together for a stronger bond and a better understanding of each other. SNAP expands into four individual projects, each with their own uniqueness and mission towards spreading love and support for neurodiversity.

SNAP App

Gives neurodiverse individuals a new system to find others like-minded and develop friendships from across the world.

SNAP Research

Finding the best social intervention to help Autism Spectrum Disorder Adolescence with their social impairment and adjusting to neurotypical settings

See Abstract Learn More

SRI

The SNAP Research Initiative will allow for greater discoveries to help all neurodiverse individuals with their social compliance and adjusting to a more neurotypical-based setting.
