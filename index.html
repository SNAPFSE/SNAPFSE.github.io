Welcome to SNAP!

The Special Needs Assistance Program (SNAP) for Social Engagement was created to increase the engagement, awareness, and communication between neurodiverse and neurotypical people, bringing both groups together to create a stronger bond and better understanding of each other.

SNAP Research

We aim to create a communication tool for neurotypical and neurodiverse individuals based on the double empathy problem to improve communication, awareness, and understanding between the two communities.

Learn more about SNAP Research

The SNAP Club

The SNAP Club at the University of Pittsburgh is a student organization with a mission to educate and inform the neurotypical population about the neurodiverse community and provide a platform for interaction and communication between the two communities.
