Welcome to SNAP!

The Special Needs Assistance Program (SNAP) for Social Engagement was created to increase the engagement, awareness, and communication between neurodiverse and neurotypical people, bringing both groups together to create a stronger bond and better understanding of each other. SNAP expands into four individual projects, each with its unique mission to spread love and support for neurodiversity.

SNAP Research

We aim to find the best social intervention to help Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) adolescents with their social impairment and struggle to adjust to neurotypical settings.
